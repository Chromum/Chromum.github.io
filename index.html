About Me

I am a games programmer currently studying at Leeds City College. I Have over 5years experience using the Unity Engine dabbleing in Java and C#. Mainly focusing on the use of C# within the engine within the past few years, my main focus is creating VR experiences, AI systems and first person shooters. Throughout the time i have spent at Leeds City College i have learnt to use Blueprints in the Unreal Engine creating little games as Projects. The project i have spent the most time developing is Wormega a First Person rougelike this shows off some of my best work so be sure to check it out!
